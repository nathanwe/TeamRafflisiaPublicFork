# **GTXDankGameEngine** #
### **Sandbox controls:** ###

### **Keyboard:** ###
* Press 1：create lions badly
* Press 2: toggles the menu
* Press 3: deletes lions
* Press WASD: move camera & pokeball!
* Press <- and ->: switch scene

### **Mouse:** ###
* right click and drag change camera
